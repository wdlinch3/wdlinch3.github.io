Research Themes

Effective actions and singularities

Singularities, Born-Infeld structure, and the idea that breakdowns in a theory can point toward a more coherent effective description.

Compactification and flux backgrounds

Hidden dimensions, Calabi-Yau geometry, moduli, fluxes, branes, holography, and the covariant-superstring questions that arise when compactification is treated as a research problem rather than only a geometric picture.

Supersymmetry and off-shell structure

Off-shell supersymmetry, the limitations of existing formalisms, and the search for structures strong enough to control high-supercharge theories.

Branes, worldvolumes, and extended geometry

Worldvolume-first approaches to higher-dimensional theories and the geometries that become natural once extended objects are taken seriously.

A-theory and exceptional structures

Attempts to formulate a disciplined framework for M-theory and related exceptional structures without giving up logical control.

Fractional superspace

Hybrid and fractional superspace constructions motivated by the need for better off-shell descriptions of supersymmetric theories.

Topological field theory and strings

Mathematical structures that clarify how fields, strings, defects, and geometry fit into a broader conceptual picture.
